Kaduna, Nigeria

Kaduna is a city in Nigeria known for its rich history and cultural heritage. It is known for being a major commercial and industrial hub in Northern Nigeria, an "academic center" nicknamed the "Centre of Learning" due to its numerous educational institutions like the Nigerian Defence Academy. It is also recognized for its agricultural hub, vibrant markets, and natural attractions like Kajuru Castle and the Kagoro Hills.
